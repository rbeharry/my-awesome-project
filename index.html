About Me

Hello! I’m a front-end developer based in Germantown, MD. This page highlights my skills in building responsive, user-friendly webpages with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Feel free to explore my recent project—a dynamic weather app I designed and built.

My passion for web development began in high school, where I spent countless hours teaching myself to code on my personal computer. After college, I joined the federal government, managing the website for a small agency in Rockville, MD. When my role ended in July 2025, I decided to refresh and expand my skills. I enrolled in SheCodes, a program dedicated to empowering women in tech, where I’ve been honing my ability to design and code modern websites. This experience has reignited my creativity and strengthened my confidence in expressing ideas through code.

I’m excited to continue learning and will be showcasing more projects as I progress. If you’re looking for help with your web-related needs, I’d love to collaborate! 😀👷🏼‍♀️
