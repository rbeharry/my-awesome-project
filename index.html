About Me

I’m a front-end developer based in Germantown, MD. This page highlights my skills in building responsive, user-friendly webpages with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and API integration. Feel free to explore the projects that I designed and built.

When my role ended in July 2025, I decided to refresh and expand my skills by enrolling in SheCodes. This experience has reignited my creativity and strengthened my confidence in expressing ideas through code.

I’m excited to continue learning and will be showcasing more projects as I progress. If you’re looking for help with your web-related needs, I’d love to collaborate! 😀👷🏼‍♀️
